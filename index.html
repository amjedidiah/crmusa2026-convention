About the Convention

Five Days of Renewal,
Worship & Community

The CRM National Convention is our annual gathering of believers from across the United States and beyond — coming together for powerful worship, Spirit-led teaching, healing prayer, and the deepening of our charismatic faith. Whether you are new to the renewal or have walked in it for decades, this is a week set apart.


Join tens of brothers and sisters as we seek a fresh outpouring of the Holy Spirit, encounter God's presence together, and return home renewed and equipped for Kingdom work.
