Accessibility Statement

Effective: May 2026 · Charismatic Renewal Ministries USA (CRM USA)

CRM USA aims to make the convention registration site usable for as many people as possible, including users of assistive technologies, keyboard navigation, and reduced motion preferences.

What we strive for

  • Semantic structure (landmarks, headings, labeled forms).
  • Visible focus indicators and a skip link to main content.
  • Reduced-motion support for decorative animations.
  • Descriptive titles for embedded payment maps and third-party checkout frames where we control markup.

Third-party content

Embedded maps and payment providers (for example Zeffy) have their own accessibility characteristics. If you cannot complete a step in an embed, use the “open in new tab” option where provided or contact us for assistance.

Feedback

If you encounter a barrier on this site, please email convention@crmusanational.org with a description of the page and assistive technology used. We will try to provide an alternate path or timeline for improvement.

This statement describes current intent and practices; it is not a legal conformance certification.
